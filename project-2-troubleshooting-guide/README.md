# Troubleshooting "Account Connection" Errors in the PulsePath mobile app

## Overview
This guide explains how to troubleshoot account connection errors in the PulsePath mobile app.

## Common Signs of a Connection Error
You may be experiencing a connection error if:
- The sign-in page does not load
- The app shows an error message after login
- The account connection does not complete
- Permissions are denied during setup

## Before You Begin
Before troubleshooting, make sure:
- Your internet connection is stable
- You are using the latest version of the app
- Your third-party account username and password are correct
- Your server is running

## Troubleshooting Steps

### 1. Restart the App
Close the app completely, then reopen it and try the connection again.

### 2. Check Your Internet Connection
Make sure your device is connected to Wi-Fi or mobile data.

### 3. Update the App
Install the latest version of the app from the app store.

### 4. Reattempt the Account Connection
Go to **Settings** > **Connected Apps** and try connecting the account again.

### 5. Review Permissions
If prompted, allow the requested permissions so the connection can complete.

### 6. Try Again Later
Temporary service issues may prevent the connection from completing successfully.

### 7. Delete the app and re-download
Delete and re-download the app so the necessary prompts pop up again.

## When to Contact Support
Contact support if:
- The connection still fails after completing all troubleshooting steps
- You continue receiving the same error message
- The app closes unexpectedly during setup

## Result
After completing these steps, the account connection issue may be resolved.

## Example Error Message
You may see a message such as:
- "Connection failed"
- "Unable to complete sign-in"
- "Permissions were not granted"
- "Login username or password is incorrect"
- "404 Error"
