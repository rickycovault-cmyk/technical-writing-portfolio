# How to Connect a Music Service Account in PulsePath

## Overview
This guide explains how to connect a music service account in the PulsePath mobile app.

## Prerequisites
Before you begin, make sure you have:
- A PulsePath account
- A supported music service account
- The PulsePath app installed on your phone

## Steps
1. Open the PulsePath app.
2. Tap **Settings**.
3. Tap **Connected Apps**.
4. Select **Music Service**.
5. Tap **Connect Account**.
6. Sign in with your music service credentials.
7. Review the requested permissions.
8. Tap **Allow** to complete the connection.

## Troubleshooting
### Connection failed
If the connection fails, close the app and try again.

### Login page does not load
Check your internet connection and try again.

### Permissions were denied
Repeat the connection process and approve the requested permissions.

## Result
After completing these steps, your music service account should be connected to PulsePath.
